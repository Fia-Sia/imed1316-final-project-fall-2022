Volunteering

You can volunteer to model our clothing, accessories,and other items. Expanding on what you model can help with learning on what you are comfortable with doing and if you don't like it at least you can add it as something you did in your portfolio. You could really have a lot of fun with this and you could meet a lot of different people and make interesting connections.

You can also learn here how running a business is like and explore if it would be something you would like to do. This can be a very useful and fun experience.

And in all of this you can also learn a new hobby if it interests you or just learn something new that you never knew about before, and meet new people

Personalized

We all love clothes, accessories and other things. We all have our own style. We also like getting our loved ones something special, so that they know that we are thinking about them. We have a large variety of items to choose from. From bags, hats, book covers, toys, blankets and more. You can find something you would like or if it’s for someone you know.

But if you want something specifically made for them. Like the color,a pattern, the size, and other things. You can do it with us. You just need to contact us so we can discuss it.

You can contact us for something to be made personalized for you or your loved one. Just contact us so we can help you with that.
